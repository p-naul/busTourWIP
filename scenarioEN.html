How to navigate in 3D

  • Lelt mouse button click on bubble: zoom on component
  • Lelt mouse button click on component transfers it on your assembly desk, or sends it back
  • Lelt mouse button double-click on scene: zoom to the area
  • Mouse wheel: zoom in/out
  • Right mouse button and drift: moves right-left or up-down

How to fullfill your mission

  • Select components with Lelt mouse button click, they move to your assembly desk
  • When you think that the assembly meets your mission requirements, click on the golden globe to validate
  • If you are blocked, for whatever reason, click on button "solution" at bottom-right of scene to disclose. Then, your mission ends
  • Any time, you can quit by clicking button "Return to Missions"
